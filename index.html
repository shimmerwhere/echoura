What is Echoura?

The world's first fully accredited* (by who? don't ask) multidisciplinary institution dedicated to empowering cats in every field — from cybersecurity to cinema, from drama to data science.

Founded when someone needed to finish an assignment on Bootstrap, Echoura has grown into a structured-yet-ethereal academic universe where knowledge reverberates through cathedral-like halls and every lesson leaves an echo.
